Why use AnyForward?

Reliable Forwarding

Ensures your messages and call notifications are always delivered. With built-in retry logic, it handles network drops gracefully.

Privacy First

Your data stays local. Messages and call logs go directly from your phone to your configured email provider.

Battery Efficient

Designed to run quietly in the background without draining your battery or consuming excessive resources.

Call Notifications

Never miss an important call. Get instant email notifications for missed or rejected calls.
